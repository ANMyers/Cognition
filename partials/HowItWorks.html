How Cognition Works


Currently I'm using an algorithm called "Nearest Neighbor" or "kmeans" to take my guess, but in the future I may have more. Heres how kmeans works.



Step 1



The first step is the plot each point with the corresponding number of dimensions, for this example I have randomly generated 50 plot points at two dimensions.


Step 2



The next step is to process the data by clustering like points together, this also allows you to weigh the data depending on if there are more important attributes then others.


Step 3



The last step is where the algorithm kmeans comes into play. This algorithm will find the most defined center of your two, or more, groupings. Scroll down for the last step.
